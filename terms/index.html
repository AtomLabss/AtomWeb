    You must be at least 13 years old to use Atom. By using Atom, you represent and warrant that you meet this eligibility requirement.

    3. Description of Features

    Atom offers a variety of features to help manage your Discord server, including but not limited to:
    • Moderation tools: Automate server management tasks.
    • AI-powered responses: Provide users with intelligent, context-aware responses.
    • Contextual Memory: Saves the context and prompts from your interactions to improve the accuracy and relevance of AI-generated responses.
    • Third-party API Integration: Requests and data from AtomAI are sent to GroqAI for processing.

    4. Data Collection and Privacy

    4.1. Data Collected

    To enable Atom’s features, including the AI-powered responses, the following data may be collected:
    • Discord userIDs
    • Discord serverIDs
    • Messages and prompts sent to AtomAI
    • Contextual data to enhance user experience
    • Metadata related to bot interactions
    This data is used solely to provide and improve Atom’s features and is stored securely. Parts of your data may be shared with GroqAI, a third-party AI service, solely for the purpose of processing AI requests and generating responses.

    4.2. Data Usage

    Data collected by Atom is used to:
    • Provide accurate and context-aware responses for AtomAI
    • Improve the functionality and user experience of the bot
    • Analyze bot usage and performance

    4.3. Data Sharing

    AtomAI integrates with GroqAI to provide said AI-powered features. Parts of your data, including prompts, will be sent to GroqAI for processing. GroqAI is obligated to protect your data and comply with privacy regulations.

    4.4. Data Retention

    Your data will be retained for as long as necessary to provide Atom’s services or as required by law. You may request the deletion of your data at any time by opening a ticket at Atom Support, our Discord server.
